###Bright Coffee Shop Dashboard

#This repository contains a comprehensive Power BI Dashboard designed to track and analyze the sales performance of Bright Coffee Shop.

#The dashboard provides actionable insights into revenue trends, product popularity, and store performance across multiple locations.

## Project Overview
*The goal of this project was to transform raw coffee shop transactional data into a visual story that helps stakeholders understand business health, peak operating hours, and customer spending habits.

#Total Revenue: $698.81K

## Key Features & Visualizations
1. Revenue Trends & Distributions
*Monthly Revenue by Location: A clustered column chart showing performance peaks, specifically highlighting strong sales in June across Astoria, Hell's Kitchen, and Lower Manhattan.

*Revenue by Product Category: A donut chart illustrating that Coffee and Tea drive the majority of sales, while "Branded" items and "Bakery" contribute smaller shares.

*Revenue by Day of the Week: A bar chart revealing that Monday and Friday are the highest-earning days, suggesting steady weekday traffic.

2. Operational Insights
*Location Performance: A clear breakdown showing that Hell's Kitchen leads in total revenue, followed closely by Astoria.

*Time Classification: An area chart analyzing revenue flow through different parts of the day (Morning, Mid-Morning, Afternoon, and*Night). This helps in optimizing staff shifts.

3. Customer Behavior
*Spend Bucket Analysis: A funnel-style bar chart categorizing customers by their spending levels (Medium, Low, High, and Very High).

4. Interactive Slicers
#Users can filter the entire report by:

Store Location

*Month (Jan – Jun)

*Time Classification (Peak vs. Off-peak hours)

## Tools Used
*Power BI Desktop: For data modeling, DAX measures, and visualization.

*Data Source: Coffee shop transactional records (CSV/Excel).

*GitHub: For version control and documentation.
